BROLY

The Legendary Super Saiyan | Uncontrollable Power | Pure Rage

Unmatched, Cataclysmic Strength

**Broly** is a Saiyan survivor with a unique genetic mutation, born with a power level far exceeding even the King of all Saiyans. His immense, **uncontrollable power** manifested in the fearsome **Legendary Super Saiyan** form, where his strength and stature grow exponentially with his rage. Driven by trauma and manipulation in the original timeline, or simply raw, primal power in the modern continuity, Broly represents the absolute pinnacle of Saiyan destructive capability. He is a walking cataclysm, whose very existence threatens the stability of the universe.
