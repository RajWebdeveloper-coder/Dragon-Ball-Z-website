GOKU BLACK

Super Saiyan Rosé | God of Justice | The Zero Mortal Plan

The Exquisite Power of a God

**Goku Black** is the malevolent entity born from Zamasu's soul inhabiting Goku's body, driven by the twisted ideology of the "Zero Mortal Plan." His signature transformation, **Super Saiyan Rosé**, is the divine equivalent of Super Saiyan Blue, showcasing an awe-inspiring pink aura and immense destructive power. Black possesses all of Goku's battle genius, amplified by Zamasu's inherent divine energy, allowing him to grow stronger with every conflict. He wields unique abilities like the **Violent Fierce God Slicer** (Ki Blade) and the **Black Kamehameha**, embodying absolute confidence and an unwavering commitment to his self-proclaimed divine justice.
