DAISHINKAN

The Grand Minister | Highest Authority in the Cosmos | Father of the Angels

The Supreme Steward of Zeno

**Daishinkan**, or **The Grand Minister**, stands as one of the most powerful and important beings in the multiverse, serving as the personal attendant and guide to the Omni-King, Zeno. His true strength is said to surpass even the strongest Angels. He is the **father of all the Angels**, including Whis, Vados, and the others attending the Gods of Destruction. With a perpetually polite and serene demeanor, Daishinkan manages the affairs of the cosmic hierarchy and is the primary coordinator for events like the **Tournament of Power**. His word is absolute law, second only to Zeno himself, making him a figure of both immense respect and subtle dread.
