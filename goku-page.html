Son Goku – KAKAROT!

Limit Breaker | Super Saiyan God | Master Ultra Instinct

The Earth's Mightiest Hero

Goku, or **Kakarot**, is the last pure-blooded Saiyan survivor of Planet Vegeta's destruction, sent to Earth as an infant. Raised by Grandpa Gohan, his mission of conquest was forgotten, replaced by a pure and relentless pursuit of martial arts mastery. His signature techniques, the **Kamehameha** and the **Spirit Bomb**, embody his incredible power and selfless heart. He continuously breaks his limits, achieving transformations like Super Saiyan, Super Saiyan 3, and the divine Super Saiyan God forms, all to protect his family, friends, and his beloved planet from cosmic threats. His journey is the ultimate testament to hard work and indomitable will.
