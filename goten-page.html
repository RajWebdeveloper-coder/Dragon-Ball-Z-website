**Goten** is the youngest son of Goku, a hybrid Saiyan blessed with unimaginable natural talent. He famously achieved the **Super Saiyan** form at an incredibly young age with virtually no training, showcasing his immense, inherited power level. His defining trait is his deep bond with his best friend, **Trunks**, with whom he performs the **Fusion Dance** to create the supremely powerful, yet often cocky, fighter **Gotenks**. Though often playful and naive, his potential is seen as the future of the Z Fighters.
