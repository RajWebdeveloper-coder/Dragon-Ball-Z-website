ZENO

The Omni-King | Ruler of All 12 Universes | Child-Like Authority

Unfathomable Power, Simple Desires

**Zeno** is the supreme ruler of all existence—the **Omni-King**—who presides over the 12 Universes. Despite his small, innocent, and seemingly carefree appearance, he wields an absolute power that is beyond comprehension: the ability to instantly **erase** entire universes from existence with a single thought. His power is so immense that even the Gods of Destruction tremble in his presence. Accompanied by his two attendants, he often seeks entertainment and companionship, famously striking a simple friendship with Goku. His whimsical nature, combined with his unparalleled authority, makes him the most dangerous and revered entity in the cosmos.
