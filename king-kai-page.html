KING KAI

North Kai | Galactic Overseer | Master of Gravity Training

The Humorous God and Master Trainer

**King Kai** (formally known as North Kai) is the benevolent, yet often easily annoyed, deity overseeing the North Galaxy. Residing on a tiny planet with **10x Earth's gravity**, he is best known for being the first to train Goku in the afterlife. Under his guidance, Goku learned fundamental techniques like the **Kaio-ken** and the **Spirit Bomb**, which became cornerstones of his fighting style. Despite his divine status, King Kai is down-to-earth, obsessed with cars, and famous for his terrible puns. He maintains vital connections across the cosmos, often serving as a crucial source of information for the Z-Fighters.
