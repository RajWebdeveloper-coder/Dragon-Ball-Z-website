BARDOCK – THE AVENGER OF VEGETA

Low-Class Warrior | Saiyan Revolutionary | Father of Kakarot

The Legacy of Defiance

**Bardock** was a low-class Saiyan warrior and the biological father of Goku (Kakarot). A hardened veteran, his life was tragically cut short when he stood alone against the monstrous power of **Frieza** to prevent the destruction of Planet Vegeta. A dying Kanassan warrior gifted him with the power to see the future, turning him into the only Saiyan aware of their impending doom. Though his final strike was futile, his desperate Ki wave, the **Final Spirit Cannon**, represents the Saiyan people's final act of **unyielding courage**. He ensured his son's escape, setting in motion the prophecy that Kakarot would one day avenge their annihilated race.
