LORD BEERUS

God of Destruction | Universe 7's Deity | The Epitome of Power

Ancient Deity and Master of Balance

**Beerus** is the powerful, temperamental, and often lazy God of Destruction for Universe 7, whose primary function is to maintain cosmic balance by eliminating life, a job he often puts off for long naps. A master of **Destruction Energy (Hakai)** and trained by his attendant, **Whis**, Beerus is one of the strongest entities in all of the Universes. His greatest weakness is not a lack of power, but his love for Earth's cuisine and his deep, easily triggered, insecurity about his rival, **Champa**. His arrival marks the introduction of the divine hierarchy and cosmic stakes to the Z-Fighters' world.

WHIS

Angelic Attendant | Martial Arts Instructor | Master of Time and Space

The Zen Master and Fastest in the Universe

**Whis** serves as the **Angel Attendant** and personal martial arts master to Lord Beerus. His speed is unparalleled, capable of traveling across the universe in mere moments. More importantly, he is a being of **Ultra Instinct** mastery, a state of mind where his body reacts autonomously to any threat, making him nearly untouchable in combat. Though perpetually calm, often focused on finding the next delightful dessert, Whis holds power that far surpasses the God of Destruction he serves. He is the living embodiment of detached, effortless omnipotence, and the key to unlocking the Z-Fighters' true potential.
