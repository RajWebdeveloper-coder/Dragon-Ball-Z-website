The Earth's Smartest Fighter

**Trunks** is the son of **Vegeta** and **Bulma**, inheriting his mother's genius intellect and his father's raw Saiyan power. He is confident, sometimes arrogant, and incredibly gifted, achieving the **Super Saiyan** form at a very young age. His strongest bond is with his best friend, **Goten**, with whom he is inseparable. Together, they perform the **Fusion Dance** to form the powerful, trash-talking fighter **Gotenks**. Trunks' creativity, fueled by his mother's scientific heritage, makes him an unpredictable and formidable warrior.
