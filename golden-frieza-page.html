GOLDEN FRIEZA

Emperor of the Universe | Ultimate Evolution | Absolute Malevolence

The Ultimate Evolution of Malice

**Frieza**, the self-proclaimed Emperor of the Universe, achieved his **Golden Form** through minimal but intense training, realizing his true potential after being revived. This transformation grants him a power boost that rivals, and initially surpasses, a fully-trained Super Saiyan Blue. While susceptible to stamina drain in its early stages, the Golden Form epitomizes Frieza's capacity for ruthlessness and his unending desire for revenge against the Saiyans. He is a master tactician, a brilliant orator, and a constant, menacing presence across the cosmos.
