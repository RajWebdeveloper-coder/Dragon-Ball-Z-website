BULMA – THE EARTH'S GENIUS!

Capsule Corp CEO | Dragon Radar Inventor | Family Anchor

The Brains Behind the Power

Bulma Briefs, the brilliant daughter of Dr. Briefs and CEO of **Capsule Corp**, is the technological backbone of the Z Fighters. Her invention of the **Dragon Radar** was the catalyst for the entire *Dragon Ball* saga. Though she lacks physical power, her intelligence, charisma, and ability to build everything from time machines to new battle armor make her the single most important human on Earth. She is the proud wife of Vegeta and the mother of Trunks and Bulla, using her resources and genius to support the planet's defense.
