ANDROID 18

Wife & Mother | Z Fighter Veteran | Infinite Energy

The Fierce Protector and Unstoppable Force

**Android 18** (Lazuli) is, alongside her twin brother, a masterpiece of Dr. Gero's research, possessing an **infinite energy reactor** that negates the need for traditional training and rest. Initially a threat, she became a permanent member of the Z Fighters after marrying **Krillin** and starting a family with her daughter, **Marron**. Her intelligence, unwavering confidence, and raw power make her an invaluable asset. She excels in hand-to-hand combat and demonstrated her commitment to her universe during the **Tournament of Power**, fighting for her family's future and showcasing her true heroic spirit.
