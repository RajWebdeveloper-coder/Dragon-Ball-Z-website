PICCOLO – THE NAMEKIAN LEGEND

Earth's Guardian | Gohan's Mentor | Tactical Genius

From Villain to Valued Ally

Originally the evil King Piccolo's reincarnation, **Piccolo Jr.** chose a path of redemption, becoming one of Earth's most trusted and indispensable protectors. His stern demeanor hides a deep wisdom and a fierce loyalty, especially towards **Gohan**, whom he rigorously trained and loves like a son. His **Namekian** heritage grants him unique abilities like regeneration, stretching limbs, and enhancing hearing. Piccolo is renowned for his tactical prowess, often devising strategies that even Saiyans overlook, and his devastating **Special Beam Cannon (Makankōsappō)**.
