SUPREME KAI SHIN

Lord of Creation | Kaioshin of Universe 7 | Counterpart to Lord Beerus

The Unifier of Creation and Life

**Shin** is the youngest and sole surviving **Supreme Kai** of Universe 7, following the defeat of Majin Buu. As a Kaioshin, he is responsible for the **creation** of new life and planets, and his very life is linked to that of Lord Beerus, the God of Destruction. Despite his immense divine power, Shin is often overwhelmed by his responsibilities and fearful of stronger fighters, demonstrating humility and caution. His efforts to guide Goku and the Z-Fighters, particularly during the tournaments, highlight his dedication to protecting Universe 7, sometimes in spite of his own anxieties. He is the guardian of the life-giving force in his universe.
