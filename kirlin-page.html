Heart, Resilience, and Power

**Krillin** may be the strongest human on Earth, but his true strength lies in his **unbreakable spirit and loyalty**. Starting as Goku's competitive rival, he quickly became his closest lifelong friend and an indispensable member of the Z Fighters. Despite often facing enemies vastly superior in power, Krillin **never gives up**. He is renowned for his ingenious, tricky fighting style, his **solar flare** technique, and his razor-sharp **Destructo Disc**, an attack feared by even the most powerful villains, proving that courage often triumphs over raw power levels.
