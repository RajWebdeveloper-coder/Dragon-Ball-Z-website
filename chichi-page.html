CHI-CHI – THE OX PRINCESS!

World Tournament Finalist | Ox-King's Daughter | The Unbreakable Will

The Earth's Underrated Fighter

Chi-Chi, the **Ox-King's Daughter**, debuted as a formidable warrior, capable of striking fear into her enemies with her sharp helmet and powerful fighting style. Though her focus shifted to academics for her children, she remains one of the Earth's strongest human women. She is a **World Tournament Finalist**, a true martial arts prodigy, and her strength—both physical and psychological—is the driving force that keeps the legendary Son family grounded. Her history proves she is capable of handling any threat, domestic or cosmic.
